ABOUT US

How we came to partner:

Taste shouldn’t be the only thing you appreciate while enjoying our beer. Our food scientists are always hard at work developing new tastes to bring to our beer. They just so happened to discover a new technology that can make your clothes smell great, without sacrificing taste. That’s right! BECK’S® still has the same great taste with the added benefit of smelling great! The obvious answer was to team up with Renuzit® Brand Snuggle® Fabric Refresher, the professionals of fresh smelling clothes. We knew immediately we had to get this new beer into the hands of our customers with the highest standard of taste and smell.
